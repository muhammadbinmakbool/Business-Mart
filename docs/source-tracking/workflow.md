# Source Tracking Workflow

**Optional Informational Layer**

The workflow for Source Tracking is strictly manual and decoupled from the sales entry process.

## 1. Fast Entry (Sales)
Sales are recorded normally in the **Sales / Billing** module. Speed is prioritized. No mapping is required at the time of sale.

## 2. Register Entry (Manual)
When the business owner or clerk has time, they navigate to the **Source Tracking** module to log mappings.

**Steps:**
1. Click **New Mapping Entry**.
2. Optionally select a **Sale #** or **Intake #** to associate the entry.
3. Optionally select **Supplier** and **Buyer**.
4. Enter the **Quantity** and (optionally) the **Rates**.
5. Save the entry.

## 3. Reviewing Mappings
The Source Tracking list view serves as a master register. It can be used to:
- Identify which intakes have been mapped to sales.
- Track realized margins (Buying Rate vs Selling Rate).
- Support future settlement discussions with suppliers.

## Business Flexibility
If a business does not use this module, they can simply ignore it or disable it in the UI. The core system's inventory and financial integrity remain perfectly intact.
